About GeoGuard

A web application designed to assist users and governments in preparing for and reallocating resources for flood events

Floods are among the most perilous and frequent natural disasters globally.

Annually, floods claim thousands of lives and displace millions, stripping them of their homes and livelihoods. Notably, regions such as various Indian states and China's Yellow River Valley have experienced some of the deadliest floods, resulting in massive casualties.

In response to this pressing issue, our team developed FloodGuard. This tool predicts floods and their potential impacts before they occur. The predictions are then presented in an interactive graphical format, making the data compelling and easily comprehensible for both the public and government authorities.
